Description

UHub is a centralized platform to discover and explore resources on campus.

With UHub, students can find the locations of food outlets on campus and browse their menus. With various search options available to find specific food items, dietary restrictions, and more, UHub removes the stress of finding what to eat on campus.

Stop googling for lackluster campus resources and find everything you need in a centralized user-friendly experience with more features like study space availability coming soon.

Screenshots

Key Features

  • Discover and explore food outlets on campus with a fully interactive map
  • Browse detailed menus of food outlets
  • See dietary restriction information
  • View hours of operation

Coming soon:

  • Search the menu of all food outlets simultaneously
  • Filter results by dietary restrictions, buildings, and more
  • View currently available study spaces on campus

About the Team

We are a team of four UVic students currently in a Software Engineering class on startup programming with the goal of developing an app that brings disjoint campus resources into one centralized and easy to use place. We are using this project to learn about building a new tech startup while also trying to solve some of the common problems we face daily as students on campus.
