Installation Instructions

How to install UHub

UHub is currently in early-access beta and is available for download on both iOS and Android devices.

While the app is not yet listed on the Apple App Store or Google Play Store, you can download the app by following the instructions below.

Table of Contents

  • Installing on iOS
  • Installing on Android

Installing on iOS

Required platforms: iOS or iPadOS apps: iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch running iOS 14 or iPadOS 14 or later. App Clips require iOS 14 or iPadOS 14, or later.

  1. Install TestFlight on the iOS or iPadOS device that you’ll use for testing.
  2. Open your email invitation and tap "View in TestFlight" or open this public link on your device.
  3. If you’re a new tester for the app, tap Accept.
  4. Tap Install to install the app on your device.
  5. If you’re a returning tester, tap Update or Open.

Installing on Android

To install UHub on your Android device, follow the steps below:

  1. Download the UHub .apk file on your Android device from the UHub product page.
  2. In your File Manager app, tap on the downloaded .apk file to install UHub.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation.
  4. NOTE: You may be asked to enable the "Unknown sources" permission to allow installation of apps from sources other than the Play Store.
  5. Once the installation is complete, you can find the UHub app on your Android device home screen and launch it!
